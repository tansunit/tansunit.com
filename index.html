About Me

My name is Chen Kun. I come from Shaanxi Province of China and live in Paris. I am studying for a Master’s degree at the center for research and interdisciplinarity at the University of Paris, with a focus on educational technology.

I am the co-founder of “As It Is Education” company, and I was also the last director general of Liren College.
